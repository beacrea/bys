Current Services

Logo Creation

Logo creation is probably one of the most fun projects we get, especially when it's for startups. When you start from the ground up and from a blank slate, you can always create something awesome. For new businesses, this is potentially the launching point for the next few years and their image is a critical factor in their success.

Our process for creating logos starts with a questionaire that ranges from questions about your target audience to what your company would be like if it were a celebrity. By getting a great feel on a client's vision for their company and the information from our questions, we can craft you a great look for your shiny new startup.

Like all the BYS products, we follow a formula that gets you a great logo quickly and inexpensively. We can make up to two different concepts, with two major revisions each (but usually we nail it).

When we're done, you get all the artwork (a production and final AI file + fonts). Enjoy!

Fixed Price : $250 Completion Time: 0-1.5 weeks (average)

Business Cards

We love business cards. Everything from the ink, to the stock, to the noise a card makes when you snap a corner. When you hand someone a great card, that first impression is everything. If you really want to look great as you run around getting exposure, look no further.

We typically create our artwork in illustrator or indesign. We can create for all sizes, as long as it's stated up front. We also have printers on hand to help you out so you don't have to worry.

Typically, we start off with some sketches on what the cards will look like and then move right into the design. You'll get one concept and two revisions with this one.

Fixed Price : $150 + Printing Completion Time: 0-1.5 weeks (average) Psst! We use moo.com for our business cards. Maybe you should too! =D
